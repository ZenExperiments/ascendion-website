An NMSDC-certified minority business enterprise

Ascendion is an NMSDC-certified minority business enterprise, hiring across the U.S. and Mexico. We use AI across functions to deliver for Fortune 100 companies and invest in our people through embedded learning, mentorship, and career paths built for growth.

Building in Latin America

Ascendion Latin America delivers nearshore digital engineering excellence through delivery centers in Mexico, Colombia, and Brazil, supported by a strong regional talent network. Since launching in Monterrey in March 2023, LATAM has become central to Ascendion's mission, scaling platform engineering, data, and Agentic AI capabilities for global enterprises.
