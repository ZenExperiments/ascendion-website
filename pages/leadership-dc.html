Board of Directors

Debashis (DC) Chatterjee

Board Member

Debashis Chatterjee ("DC") is one of the most recognized leaders in global technology services and a driving force in shaping the future of the industry. With over three decades of experience building and transforming billion-dollar enterprises, he brings unmatched strategic and operational expertise to Ascendion's Board of Directors.

As CEO and Managing Director, DC led the landmark merger of Mindtree and LTI to create LTIMindtree, India's sixth-largest IT services company. Earlier, during more than two decades at Cognizant, he built the Banking & Financial Services business into the company's largest vertical and led global delivery operations serving clients worldwide.

Renowned for building high-performance teams, driving enterprise-wide change, and forging alliances that last, DC now helps guide Ascendion's mission to lead in AI-powered software engineering—anchored in velocity, trust, and capital efficiency.
